# The Heliacon manifesto

Heliacon is a studio and a consultancy. Its spine is an origin.

An origin is the canonical source of an organisation's identity, knowledge, capability and trust. Everything else is a projection of it. A website is one projection. A JSON document is another. An answer an agent gives about you is another. Most companies pour their effort into the projections and let the origin rot. We do the opposite.

## The belief

The browser is not privileged. Neither is the agent. For thirty years the web assumed a human with a screen. That assumption is ending. The next reader of your work is as likely to be a model assembling an answer as a person scrolling a page. It has no patience for layout and no way to judge a pretty site. It needs the source as data, and it needs to know where that source came from.

So we build origin-first. One canonical source of truth, published in whatever form the consumer can use, each carrying its own provenance. Who said it, from what, and when, attached to the claim itself.

## What we hold

- **Provenance by default.** Nothing is authoritative without a traceable path back to what substantiates it.
- **Privacy by architecture.** Not a policy bolted on. A property of how the thing is built.
- **Capability over content.** An origin is judged by what it can do, not by how much it publishes.
- **Invocation over attention.** We measure success by use, not by traffic. Being invoked beats being seen.
- **Sovereignty.** You hold your origin. No one rents it to you.

## The three faces, one origin

- **The research explains.** A canonical corpus of definitions, architecture and patterns for being found, trusted and invoked in an agentic world. This is the origin, and it is the credibility everything else rests on.
- **The products prove.** Apps, tools and games built on the research. Apps prove. Games explore. Tools expose. Crawl Space is the tool that embodies the work on machine answerability. Each product is a projection of the same origin.
- **The consulting applies.** We help other companies become origin-first. The same architecture, brought to your work, so that humans, software and future systems can all consume one source of truth.

Research explains. Products prove. Consulting applies. One origin, many projections.

## Why it is built this way

Because architecture outlives fashion. We do not tie Heliacon's identity to today's language for these things. The phrase for the agentic web will date. The idea of a single canonical source, projected for whoever asks and carrying its own provenance, will not.

Because trust is the scarce thing. When machines synthesise answers from a dozen sources, the source that can be verified is worth more than the source that shouts loudest.

Because the discipline is the product. Every change answers one question. Does this make the origin more canonical. Not, does this make the website prettier.

## Be first light

Heliacal rising is the moment a star returns to the dawn sky after its season out of sight. First light. That is the intent. To be the thing that rises first and is trusted when it does.

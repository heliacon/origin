# About

Heliacon is me, Pete Dainty.

## Why I started Heliacon

The web is being read by machines as much as by people now. Most companies still write only for the browser and quietly go invisible to the models and agents that increasingly decide what gets found, trusted and used. I started Heliacon to fix that from the origin up.

## How I work

Substance over spin. I measure by what can be proven on the wire, not what sounds good in a slide. You get the research, the reference implementation and the tools behind it, applied to your problem.

This site is built the way I build. One canonical origin, projected for humans and machines, every claim carrying its provenance. It runs a working MCP server, a citation-first ask endpoint and a provenance API, and it is source-available. Do not take my word for any of it. Inspect it.

I take a small number of engagements at a time, because senior attention does not scale and I would rather do a few things properly.
